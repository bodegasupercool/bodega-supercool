about

WE ARE BODEGA SUPERCOOL

We are Fernando & Paige, a New York couple who fell in love through technology, art, and humor. Through Bodega SuperCool, we aim to explore changing perspectives on commerce, community, and definitions of ownership. We strive to infuse life into static concepts, crafting captivating narratives that resonate with audiences.

Whether you're a start-up looking to establish your brand identity, or an established business aiming to enhance your visual presence, we are here to transform your ideas into immersive, awe-inspiring realities.

Fernando is a freelance 3D & motion artist focused on blending technology and creativity. Paige works in technology as a Product Manager.
